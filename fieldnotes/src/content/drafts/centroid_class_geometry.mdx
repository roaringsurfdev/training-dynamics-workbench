What I expected:
To see the internal geometry evolve from a point cloud to clear internal ring geometry during second descent.

What we actually see:
Ring geometry can show up as early as epoch 500 in some models, which is clearly within first descent.
Instead of steady refinement during second descent, the model appears to be expanding to more dimensions in order to reconstruct and reorganize its internal representation.
Instead of a clear ring developing during second descent, we observe a model starting with ring geometry and then proceeding through Lissajous-like transformations during the second descent test loss.
Models can experience geometric disturbances that remain throughout the course of training, which does not surface on the loss curve.
