This is a research notebook, not a publication venue.

The work happens first — training runs, gradient plots, PCA decompositions, interventions — and writing follows when something is worth naming. These entries will be short, direct, and in progress. Some will be polished later. Many won't need to be.

The subject is mechanistic interpretability for transformer training dynamics: what does a model actually learn, and how does learning happen? Not *whether* it learned — but the shape of the process.

The platform behind this work is [MIScope](https://github.com/roaringsurfdev/miscope), a dynamics analysis workbench built to standardize and evolve lenses on models as they train.

More to come.
